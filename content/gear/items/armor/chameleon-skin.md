*Form-fitting suit with adaptive camouflage, sound-dampening, and thermal masking.*

Minimal physical protection but excels at reducing sensor signature for infiltration.

**Effect:** Reduces incoming Physical Harm by 1 level (minimum 1). Reduces effectiveness of sensor-based detection (visual, thermal, EM, audio — GM determines mechanical effect, likely increased TN for detection checks).

**Binding rationale:** Ghost 2 — requires constant mental adjustment and predictive modeling to optimize camouflage patterns and mask signatures against active scanning.
