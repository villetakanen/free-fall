*Full-body powered armor plating for frontline assaults.*

Maximum kinetic protection using layered composites and limited servo-assistance. Visibly bulky and intimidating.

**Effect:** Reduces incoming Physical Harm by 3 levels (minimum 1). Hardened: the first Break Bound Gear result makes it Faulty instead of Broken.

**Binding rationale:** Body 3 — significant weight and rigidity greatly impede natural movement, requiring strength to operate effectively.
