*A load-bearing harness with multiple attachment points.*

Designed to help carry heavy equipment more comfortably by distributing weight.

**Effect:** Ignores up to 3 points of Binding Cost from gear attached to it.

**Binding rationale:** Body 1 — basic encumbrance of the harness itself.
