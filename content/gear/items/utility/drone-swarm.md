*Deployable network of micro-drones providing distributed sensory input and limited remote interaction.*

Controlled via neural link or Operator's Slate. Provides eyes and hands at a distance, but is fragile.

**Effect:** Enables remote observation up to Medium range and simple interactions (pressing buttons, carrying small objects). Susceptible to disruption (area effects, EW, concentrated fire), potentially triggering Limited Use.

**Binding rationale:** Ghost 3 — coordinating a swarm, processing multiple sensory feeds simultaneously, and maintaining control under potential EW interference is extremely taxing on focus and predictive ability.
