*A concealed emitter, usually in the wrist or fingertip, projects a near-invisible monomolecular filament.*

A covert weapon system integrated into the user's limb. Projects a filament whip for devastating melee attacks.

**Effect:** As an action, projects the monowire. Attacks made with it have DV 3 (Physical), High Impact, and are Covert. Requires an action to retract.

**Binding rationale:** Ghost 1 — requires constant low-level mental adjustment to maintain filament tension and control, taxing focus and composure.
