*Genetically tweaked nerve clusters and micro-relays enhance reflex arcs.*

Enhanced nerve conduction pathways significantly reduce reaction latency.

**Effect:** Enhanced reaction times. If the character is Surprised, they ignore the normal initiative penalties; they resolve their action first during the turn's resolution phase (meaning they declare their action last).

**Binding rationale:** Mind 1 — constant low-level processing required to manage the accelerated signaling without causing jitter or overload.
