*A synthetic gland integrated near the kidneys floods the system with potent combat stimulants on command.*

A small, internally implanted gland that can trigger a massive dump of tailored hormones and stimulants.

**Effect:** Once per Regroup, the user can trigger the gland as an action. Restore all spent Current Body points, bringing Current Body up to its maximum value (Permanent minus Binding Costs).

**Binding rationale:** Ghost 1 — represents the conscious effort to override natural limits and the subsequent jarring return to baseline, taxing willpower and composure.
