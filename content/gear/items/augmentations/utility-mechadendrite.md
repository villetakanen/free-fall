*A retractable, multi-jointed mechanical arm mounted on the user's back or torso, tipped with interchangeable tools.*

A versatile mechanical limb providing an extra point of interaction. Requires separate tool head modules (manipulator claw, cutting torch, diagnostic probe — treated as separate Gear items). The base mount is Invasive; tool heads are Field-Operable and swappable during Regroup.

**Effect:** Allows interaction with objects at short range without using organic limbs. Requires appropriate tool head Gear to perform specific tasks.

**Binding rationale:** Ghost 2 for the constant neural load of the base integration and spatial awareness. Mind 2 for the focus required during active control and manipulation.
