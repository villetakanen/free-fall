*A standard cybernetic data port, typically wrist or temple-mounted, with accompanying neural inductance hardware.*

Provides a direct machine interface port and accompanying neural implants. Allows direct connection and mental interaction with compatible systems (computers, drones, vehicles).

**Effect:** Enables direct mental interaction with compatible systems, potentially bypassing standard interfaces. The Load Bearing quality negates up to 8 points of Mind Binding cost specifically from software or systems accessed *through this link*, mitigating the cognitive load of complex operations run via the interface.

**Binding rationale:** Ghost 3 — deep integration with the nervous system required for direct machine interface imposes significant strain on the user's sense of self and mental stability.
