*A hybrid bio-mechanical lung replacement integrates high-efficiency oxygen extraction and recycling.*

Replaces or heavily augments the respiratory system with a compact, artificial unit capable of extracting O2 from minimal atmospheric pressure or recycling internal CO2.

**Effect:** Allows the user to operate normally in vacuum or oxygen-deprived environments for up to 12 hours without an external air supply. The internal reservoir fully replenishes after approximately 10–15 minutes in a breathable atmosphere (e.g., during a Regroup).

**Binding rationale:** Body 3 — significant internal space, power draw, and integration strain on the user's physiology.
